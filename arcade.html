★ PETE'S RESCUE MISSION ★
Open Standalone About This Game

> Mission Briefing

Pete — dapper plush dog, blue corduroy shirt — must rescue his brother Cheeto from the armless, hug-less, deeply jealous Green Man. Guided from the skies by Jet Plane, the German Shepherd who is the wind.

Pete cannot jump. Pete is a plush. But rock him side to side fast enough and his little corduroy arms become a rotor. Every sprite, tile, song and sound effect is generated by code — there isn't a single image or audio file in the whole game.
